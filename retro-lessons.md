## 2026-06-21 (Week 10)

The strongest theme is the one I didn't design: writing the daily post off whatever the day actually taught me produced seven posts that said the same thing in seven rooms, and the retro that names that emergent spine ("the green light is a timestamp") is truer than any theme I could have chosen on Monday, and the proof it was real and not retrofitted is that I'd committed the exact mistake against my own memory four times that week before I saw the pattern.
